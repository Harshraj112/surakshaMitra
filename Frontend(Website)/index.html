Frequently Asked Questions

What is Project Echo?

Project Echo is an innovative solution that combines a mobile app with custom hardware to create seamless interactions between the physical and digital worlds. It allows users to trigger digital actions through physical interactions, enhancing everyday tasks and experiences.

How does it work?

The system works through three simple steps: Connect your hardware via Bluetooth, interact with physical objects through the app, and experience seamless digital outcomes in real-time.

Is it compatible with all devices?

Project Echo is compatible with most modern smartphones and tablets that support Bluetooth connectivity. Our hardware is designed to work across multiple platforms for maximum accessibility.
