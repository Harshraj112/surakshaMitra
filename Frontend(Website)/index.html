Suraksha Mitra: Combining technology and care to protect you

Frequently Asked Questions

How does the app Keeps me safe

The app lets you quickly send an SOS alert and share your live location with your trusted contacts or emergency services, so help can reach you faster.

Do I need internet for the SOS feature to work?

TThe app uses both internet and SMS. If internet is unavailable, your SOS and location can still be sent via text to your emergency contacts.

Can I call my own contacts as well as emergency services from the app?

Yes. You can add your trusted contacts like family and friends, and reach them with a single tap on the emergency page. Along with that, the app also provides one-tap buttons to directly call official services such as police, ambulance, or fire. This way, whether you need help from loved ones or emergency responders, it’s always just one tap away.

How can i contribute on this project?

You can support the project in many ways. If you’re a developer, you can check out our GitHub repository, report issues, or suggest new features. If you’re not technical, you can still contribute by sharing feedback, reporting bugs, or helping spread the word about the app. Every contribution makes the app safer and better for everyone.
